i20 Latest Update
Latest Update: The third-gen i20 could soon get a new base-spec Era variant.

Hyundai i20 Price: Hyundai sells the i20 2020 from Rs 6.79 lakh to Rs 11.32 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).

Hyundai i20 Variants: It is available in four variants: Magna, Sportz, Asta and Asta (O).

Hyundai i20 Powertrains: The new i20 comes with the same three engines as the Venue: two petrol and a diesel. The first petrol unit is a 1.2-litre naturally aspirated engine that puts out 83PS and 114Nm and the second is a 1.0-litre turbo petrol, in 120PS/172Nm tune. It also gets a 1.5-litre diesel engine rated at 100PS/240Nm. While the 1.2-litre petrol engine comes mated to either a 5-speed MT or CVT, the 1.0-litre turbo-petrol unit gets either a 6-speed iMT clutchless manual or the 7-speed DCT like the Venue. The diesel engine, on the other hand, isn’t offered with an automatic gearbox and comes paired to a 6-speed MT.

Hyundai i20 Features: It gets LED headlamps, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, an air purifier, BlueLink connected car tech, and a sunroof.

Hyundai i20 Safety: Safety features include six airbags, rear parking sensors, and electronic stability control.

Hyundai i20 Rivals: Hyundai’s premium hatchback fights it out with the Honda Jazz, Tata Altroz, Volkswagen Polo, Maruti Baleno and Toyota Glanza.
